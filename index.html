Mission Statement
Team Goals

Our Goal on this journey is three things: we want to show you guys food areas near you that you may have never heard of and could give a shot. we also want to work with small business's during this time to use our platform to raise awareness of these small businesses covid-19 impacted alot of businesses so i aim to bring awareness to them whether a good or bad review we just want to show our love and appreciation for every meal. and lastly with our platform we plan to feed the less fortunate and give back to the community every chance we get; life is hard so we just want to be the people who show others that you can count on us to help you out the best we can.
